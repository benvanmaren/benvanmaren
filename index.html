About Me

I was born in Tucson, AZ, and have lived in Arizona my entire life. From a young age, the sheer mysteriousness of the world has captured my attention. I completed my undergraduate career at University of Arizona, stunned by the allure of molecular biology and science. Right now, I am working in the healthcare field with people who live with HIV, full of fulfillment and joy. Every day I am amazed at the feeling selfless kindness brings to myself and others. Still mystified like when I was younger, I am also currently exploring computer coding, world travel, and different wordly forms of self-care and health.

I am a person who is modest and caring at heart. I am proud of my accomplishments, and yet never been fully satisfied with anything--I see a piece of effort for the ways it can be improved. Wielded with my fevert curiosity and attraction to interdisciplinarity, I use my strong work ethic and rigor to widen my creative, intellectual perspectives. Indeed, I am highly passionate for healthcare; that said, I am forever motivated to learn new skills and opinions no matter how different from my pre-existing fascinations.
